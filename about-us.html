For over 37+ years, Lookad has been the largest independent media agency in Eastern India, a fortress in media buying, planning, and releasing. Our vision, however, extends beyond borders, founded upon one singular aspiration - to rank among the best media agencies in India. Lookad doesn’t just work for brands; we work with them - serving as extended partners to their core teams, understanding where to place your brand and how to make it tick.

Many advertising agencies in Kolkata boast of a big clientele, but what if ‘big’ also meant ‘valuable’? When we say we have 100+ clients, the ‘+’ signifies not just numbers, but the trust and growth we’ve facilitated. We take pride in guiding brands from their nascent stages to becoming multi-baggers listed on IPO stock exchanges. Quality lives here as a lifetime resident, and our focus on delivering results-driven media excellence defines our ethos. At LookAd, we don’t just place ads; we build brand legacies. The Look You Can’t Overlook is one that delivers results.
